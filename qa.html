How often should I water my rooftop tomato plants in summer?

I've recently started a small rooftop garden with tomato plants. With the summer heat, I'm not sure how often to water them. The plants get about 6 hours of direct sunlight daily. Should I water daily or every other day?

tomatoes watering summer-care
JohnDoe · asked 2 days ago
5 answers
SarahGreen Expert

answered 1 day ago

For rooftop tomatoes in summer, I recommend watering daily in the early morning. The key is to check the soil moisture - it should be moist but not soggy. Stick your finger about an inch into the soil; if it feels dry, it's time to water. Consider adding mulch to retain moisture.
